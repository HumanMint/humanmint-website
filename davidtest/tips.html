Residents and vistors can park in the Green, Seneca, and Cayuga Street garages and walk to anywhere in Downtown Ithaca. Garage parking is $1.00/hour in the garages. On-street parking is $1.50/hour during the week until 6pm.

If you don't want to drive, you could check the TCAT bus routs and schedules below.

Peak Hours

Keep in mind that our peak hours are from 12-2PM.

Bring Cash!

Some stands do not accept card. So make sure to bring enough cash!
